The Merge tool allows you to merge contents from different files.
1. The two panes display the files you want to merge.
2. The left pane contains your file.
3. The right pane contains the file you want to merge into yours.
4. The differences in the files are highlighted by colored bands.
5. Click on any colored band in the right pane to merge it
into your file.
6. Click 'DONE' to save the changes into your file.
