The Merge tool allows you to merge contents from different files.
1. The three panes display the files you want to merge.
2. The left-most pane contains your file.
3. The middle-pane contains the common ancestor file.
4. The right-most pane contains the file you want to merge into yours.
5. The differences in the files are highlighted by colored bands.
6. Click on any colored band in the right or on the centre pane to merge it
   into your file. 
7. Click 'DONE' to save the changes into your file.
